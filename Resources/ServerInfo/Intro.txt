Welcome to Hyperion. You've woken up on the frontier of the Hyperion Sector, on the edge of civilized space.

Hyperion is an 18+, PvE-focused ship fork descended from Monolith. It's early and under active development. The long-term goal is a living sector economy: persistent ships you keep and modify, a materials-driven market that reacts to real supply and demand, and mining, cargo, science, and medical loops worth investing time into. The most profitable ground sits at the edges, held by hostile drones.

Read the server rules (F1). For questions, check this guide, ask in-sector or over radio, or open admin help (Escape > Admin Help). Use ahelp if you see someone breaking the rules.

The sector is dangerous and persistent. Expect setbacks and plan around them.

Instead of click intents we use combat mode (keybind in controls). You can't attack anyone with it off.
